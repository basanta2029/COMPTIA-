Security Pro 8.0

10.0 Protocol, App, and Cloud Security

As you progress through this module, you'll discover how contemporary IT environments depend on virtualization, cloud computing, mobile integration, and advanced networking technologies. You will encounter real-world scenarios that highlight both the vast opportunities and unique security challenges posed by adopting these popular technologies. Through a blend of conceptual explanations and practical insights, this course empowers you to make smarter, security-focused decisions, no matter your current level of experience.

Throughout the module, you will develop the skills necessary to safeguard today's diverse IT ecosystems. From understanding how to securely deploy and manage virtual machines, to identifying threats targeting mobile devices and BYOD policies, your knowledge will expand across a variety of essential domains. By focusing on current industry practices and emphasizing actionable security strategies, this module ensures you are prepared to protect organizational assets in increasingly dynamic and interconnected environments.
In this module, you will:
Explain the fundamentals of host virtualization and identify associated security considerations.
Describe virtual networking concepts and evaluate common threats and countermeasures.
Analyze the benefits and challenges associated with deploying cloud services in an organization.
Recognize key security approaches for managing mobile devices and enforcing BYOD policies.
Identify security risks unique to embedded and specialized systems, as well as best practices for email security.
